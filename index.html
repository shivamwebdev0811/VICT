Welcome to Vidya Institute of Creative Teaching

Services

Library

The Library has a collection of over 89,000 books subscribed to journals, magazines and newspapers. Apart from this more than 13000 book titles with 75218 volumes and 18182 online international journals are available in the databases subscribed by the Library. The non-book materials collection includes 3000 CDs, 100 VCDs.

Hostel

VKP provides hostel facilities and accommodation for its staff and students in peaceful and serene environment, far distant from the noise and other pollution. The architecture is modern with wide windows to provide the natural light and air ventilation.

VISA

Vidya International Sports Academy, VISA is a fully equipped state-of-the-art games training facility for the students.

Academics

Department of management and computer application

Bachelors in Computer Application

The Department of Computer Application envisions imparting high eminence skills in software development to excel in Software Industry and to train the students to develop the soft skills with global standards and enhance their knowledge in Information Technology.

Bachelors in Business Adminitration

Basic management and business skills help in laying the foundation of the future of tomorrow. It is where the Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) degree is conducive. The course divided in six semesters an early start to a rewarding management career.

Bachelors in Commerce

This undergraduate degree program in commerce aims to provide wide range of managerial skills across various areas of business and builds a strong foundation required to take on the accelerated programs and diversified jobs in the industry.
